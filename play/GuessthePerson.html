Guess the person

2 Player Games Classic Games

Guess the person is a game where you you have to take turns asking yes or no questions to find your opponents character before they find yours.

to play each player should open the game in a seprate tab or device. your not allowed to see eachothers tabs. (make sure you both pick the same board)
you then take turns asking yes or no qustions, and clicking on people it cant be untill theres only one left, if thats their character you win
